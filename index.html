😉 About Me

Ahmed Ameen Fateh received his Ph.D. in Biomedical Engineering. His research focuses on neuroimaging, resting-state fMRI, machine learning, and computational psychiatry.

His work explores brain connectivity patterns in psychiatric and neurological disorders, aiming to develop data-driven diagnostic and predictive models.
