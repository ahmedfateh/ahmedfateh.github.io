😉 About Me

Ahmed Ameen Fateh received the B.Sc. degree in Biomedical Engineering from the Lebanese International University, Yemen, the M.Sc. degree in Biomedical Engineering from the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China, and the Ph.D. degree in Biomedical Engineering from the same institution.

He worked as a Research Fellow in the Department of Radiology at Shenzhen Children’s Hospital, Shenzhen, China. His research focuses on neuroimaging, resting-state fMRI, machine learning, dynamic functional connectivity, and computational psychiatry.

His current academic interests include brain connectivity analysis in psychiatric and neurological disorders, the integration of neuroimaging with machine learning models, and translational applications in mental health research.
